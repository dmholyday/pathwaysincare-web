Guiding Families Through Aged Care with Clarity and Compassion

When your loved one needs aged care, it can be a very stressful and overwhelming time.

Wait lists, forms, costs, confusion, pressure from hospitals, all while trying to support a vulnerable older person going through a tough, life changing transition.

We understand the system and can take the confusion and time wasting away.

With strong advocacy and expert support, we can help your loved one find the care that suits their unique needs, whether at home or in residential aged care.

How Can We Help?

At Pathways in Care, we navigate the complexities of Aged Care for you. With over 15 years of Hospital Social Work experience, we know the aged care system inside and out — cutting through red tape, explaining complex processes and costs so it makes sense, and advocating fiercely for your family's needs.

We handle the large amount of paperwork, apply to as many appropriate Aged Care Homes as necessary, and guide you through every decision with clarity and compassion.

We work for you and your loved one, to get you the outcome you need as soon as possible. No commissions, no conflicts of interest — just trusted expertise when you need it most.

Our goal is to make the process as stress free as possible and give you back the time to focus on what truly matters: being present for your loved one during this challenging transition.
