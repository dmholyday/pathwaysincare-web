The aged care system isn't designed for families in crisis. When your loved one's health declines, you're suddenly thrust into a maze of assessments, waitlists, and bureaucracy — all while costs mount and time runs out.

Navigating My Aged Care, coordinating multiple healthcare providers, deciphering complex fee structures, and completing endless paperwork shouldn't fall on families already stretched to their limit. Yet that's exactly what happens when you need care the most.

The emotional toll is immense: making life-changing decisions under pressure, managing family conflicts over care options, and carrying the weight of responsibility while watching someone you love struggle with independence and dignity.

At Pathways in Care, we step in where the system fails you. With over 15 years of Hospital Social Work experience, we know the aged care maze inside and out — cutting through red tape, translating complex processes, and advocating fiercely for your family's needs.

We handle the overwhelming paperwork, coordinate with multiple providers, negotiate fees on your behalf, and guide you through every decision with clarity and compassion. No commissions, no conflicts of interest — just trusted expertise when you need it most.

From urgent hospital discharge planning to long-term home care solutions, we give you back your time to focus on what truly matters: being present for your loved one during this challenging transition.
